Digital Growth, SEM & Web Optimization Proposal

Project Overview & Scope

As ANAYA transitions into Q2, the strategic focus shifts strictly to Growth / Momentum. To support the "ANAYA IS THE ANSWER" campaign, this proposal outlines a comprehensive digital ecosystem. We will build a high-converting SEM funnel and provide ongoing web development to ensure all premium events, from the AMA Madrid collaboration to the exclusive SOHO House rituals, are flawlessly integrated and trackable.

Phase 1: SEM & Funnel Setup (Month 1 Sprint)

Timeline: 2 Weeks
Scope: One-time comprehensive setup to establish the digital infrastructure.

This phase lays the groundwork for all future campaigns, ensuring we can track user behavior from the first ad click to the final class booking.

  • Google Ads Launchpad: Activation of an initial 400€ Google Promo credit to maximize early visibility.
  • Campaign Structuring: Building tailored ad groups based on SEO transactional keywords (e.g., "Premium Pilates Ibiza", "Lagree Studio").
  • Premium Copywriting: Drafting high-converting headlines and descriptions that reflect the brand's luxurious and exclusive tone.
  • Audience Segmentation & Targeting: Configuring demographics to capture your core personas, including the "Island Elite" and the "Second Home Woman".
  • Bidding Strategy Configuration: Setting up automated rules to maximize return on ad spend (ROAS) while maintaining premium brand positioning.
  • Conversion Tagging: Implementing Google Ads tags to track conversions, web events, and funnel drop-offs (addressing historical drop-off data).

Phase 2: The Growth Engine (4-Month Retainer)

Timeline: 4 Months (Initial Contract, covering the Q2/Q3 peak season)
Scope: Ongoing execution, web updates, and continuous optimization.

A) SEM Maintenance & Optimization

  • Ongoing Optimization: Continuous adjustments to bids, daily budgets, and negative keywords to ensure maximum efficiency.
  • A/B Testing Execution: Rigorous testing of ad texts, creative copies, and audience segmentations to find the highest-converting combinations.
  • Optimization Timeline Document: A shared, living document logging exactly what changes were made to the campaigns and when.
  • Strategy Sync: Weekly or bi-weekly follow-up calls to align on upcoming studio needs.

B) Web Development & Optimization (10 Hours/Month)

  • Offer & Pricing Updates: Updating the site to reflect Q2 pricing shifts, such as the Lagree First Class moving to 40€.
  • Event Integration: Building and updating landing pages for key Q2/Q3 activations, including AMA Madrid on April 11th, the SOHO House private wellness experience, and the Jondal July sessions.
  • Membership Management: Implementing web pop-ups and forms to transition the site to a "waiting list only" status for the closed summer memberships.
  • Media & Content: Uploading new images, videos, and texts to support the "Whatever Ibiza takes from you, ANAYA gives it back" creative direction.

*Note: New, complex web sections from scratch are outside this retainer scope; this covers dynamic updates, pop-ups, minor fixes, and landing page adjustments.

C) Local Authority: PR & Backlinks

  • Outreach Strategy: Researching and executing outreach to local Ibiza press and luxury lifestyle publications.
  • Link Building: Securing 3 to 5 high-quality backlinks per month to boost organic Domain Authority.

D) Google My Business (GMB) Management

  • Profile Updates: Publishing 2 to 3 optimized posts per month (scaling up or down based on events, new class schedules, or promo offers).
  • Review Management: Weekly monitoring and professional replies to all client reviews to maintain the brand's premium reputation.

E) Advanced Analytics & Insights

  • GA4 + Clarity Integration: Full tracking of website behavior, including heatmaps and user recordings to see how clients interact with the Mindbody widget.
  • Funnel Analysis: Tracking the complete user journey from ad impression to web landing, identifying exactly where and why users drop off before purchasing.
  • Actionable Reporting: Delivering monthly insights interpreting the data (CPC, clicks, conversions), testing demographics, and informing future strategies.
